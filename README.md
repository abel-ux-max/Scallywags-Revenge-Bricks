# Scallywag's Revenge

A pirate-themed breakout game built with HTML5 Canvas. Destroy enemy fleets, collect doubloons, and spend them on upgrades at the Bilge Rat's Store.

## How to Play

- A / D or arrow keys to move your ship
- Space to fire your cannon
- Destroy all ships to win
- Golden ships take multiple hits and reward more doubloons

## Features

- Pixel art ships with idle and movement animations
- Golden ships with damage state sprites
- Particle explosion effects
- Doubloon economy with an in-game store
- Randomized enemy fleet with flipped variants and bobbing animations
- Background music and sound effects
- SweetAlert2 dialogs for game events
